The One Piece is Real!

September 24, 2025 by Joseph Udoudo

Wealth. Fame. Power. Gol D. Roger, the king of the pirates attained this and everything else the world had to offer and his dying words drove countless souls to the seas. "You want my treasure? You can have it! I left everything I gathered together in one place now you just have to find it." These words lured men to the grand line in pursuit of dreams greater than they'd ever dared to imagine. This is the time known as the great pirate era.

Roger was the first pirate since Joy Boy to sail all the way around the Grand Line, ultimately reaching its final island, which he named Laugh Tale. Aware that he was dying from an incurable illness, he disbanded his crew and surrendered himself to be executed. Although the World Government staged his execution as a warning to deter piracy, Roger’s final words had the opposite effect, declaring that the One Piece was up for anyone who could find it, thereby sparking the Great Pirate Era.

Playboi Carti Is The Second Coming Of Shakespeare

September 24, 2025 by Joseph Udoudo

Let us look at an excerpt from a hit song from the legendary Playboi Carti called 'EVIL J0RDAN'. "First, I go whip out the boat, no, I can't hit on no brakes. My life is out of control, I'm tellin' you, nobody safe. I've been livin' my life limbo, my ice, it came with a tray. I'm so fly, I gotta hide my face, this not a rockstar phase. I'm a emo thug in my faith. Syrup, syrup, syrup, syrup, tell them to go change. Monkey nuts on me, baby, I got on two chains."

“EVIL J0RDAN” by Playboi Carti, from his album MUSIC, combines chaotic trap beats with eerie, synth-heavy atmospheres, offering a glimpse into Carti’s largely unseen lifestyle. The track opens with a futuristic yet retro soundscape that draws listeners into his dark, distorted world, quickly building into an intense, almost manic trap rhythm that matches his raw energy. The title refers to Carti’s birth name, Jordan, with the “O” replaced by a zero as a nod to his Opium label. “EVIL” reflects his portrayed persona and may also reference the YVL movement. The song’s backstory, teased on Instagram and enhanced by DJ Swamp Izzo’s haunting narration, adds to its rebellious feel, while Carti’s cousin Kee revealed it was recorded in a single night, emphasizing its spontaneous creation. Despite the rapid production, the track remains cohesive, blending classic trap with an unsettling emo-thug aesthetic unique to Carti.
